Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 stick of butter (warm, not melted)
  • 1 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • ~2 cups chocolate chips (add as much as you want!)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375¬∞F.
  2. Mix the warm butter with both sugars in a bowl until clumpy.
  3. Add eggs and vanilla; mix until smooth.
  4. Add the dry ingredients and mix well to form the dough.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips, then roll dough into balls and place on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  6. Bake for 15 minutes. Cool or enjoy fresh from the oven!

Homemade Cinnamon Rolls

Dough:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 3 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 3 tbsp butter

Filling:

  • 1/2 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp butter (melted)

Frosting:

  • 4 oz cream cheese
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup confectioners sugar

Instructions:

  1. Microwave milk and butter together, then mix with dry dough ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Knead gently, cover with a damp towel, and let rest for 15 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 350¬∞F, grease your baking pan, and mix the filling ingredients.
  4. Roll out dough on a floured surface, spread the filling, then roll it up and slice into rolls.
  5. Place rolls in the pan and bake for 25‚Äì30 minutes until golden.
  6. While baking, heat the frosting ingredients slightly and mix well.
  7. Frost the rolls after baking and enjoy warm!

Oreo Mug Cake (Beginner-Friendly Treat)

Ingredients:

  • 5 Oreo cookies
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp cocoa powder

Instructions:

  1. In a mug, crush 4 Oreos into the milk until chunky and chocolatey.
  2. Add sugar, flour, baking powder, and cocoa powder. Mix thoroughly.
  3. Press the 5th Oreo into the center of the batter.
  4. Microwave for 90 seconds. Let cool before enjoying!
