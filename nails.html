Nail Care Tips

  • Strengthen your nails: Use a strengthening base coat to keep your nails strong and healthy.
  • Take care of your cuticles: Gently push back cuticles using a cuticle pusher after moisturizing them.
  • Protect your nails: Always apply a clear top coat to protect your designs and extend their lifespan.
  • Avoid nail damage: Don't peel or bite your nails, and wear gloves when doing housework.
